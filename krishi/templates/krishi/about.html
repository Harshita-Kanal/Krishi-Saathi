About

India is an agricultural nation, A large part of India's population depends in agriculture, the farmer forms the backbone of the agricultural system. However, the condition of the Indian Farmer still remains deplorable.There is a great need to connect the farmer with technology.

The vision is to equip the farmer with accessible technology tools that would make it possible for him to alleviate his produce and at the same time provide him with all resources at one place, Precision agriculture, automated disease detection system are few of the techniques we wish to implement to improve the farmer's condition.

Other than this the motive is to empathize the citizen towards the needs of the farmers, provide farmer with a platform to connect directly with the citizen and eliminate the middleman and ensure complete transfer of profits to the farmer.This could be greatly used to alleviate the condition of the farmer
